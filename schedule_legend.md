# Color-Coded Schedule Legend

| Type / Subject    | Color      | Hex     | Based on BSIT units / rules                               |
| ----------------- | ---------- | ------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Major (with lab)  | Soft Blue  | #EDF4FF | 3-unit lab-heavy major subjects, mainly `PT` and `ITELEC` |
| Major             | Soft Violet | #F1EDFF | 3-unit core BSIT major subjects such as `MS102`           |
| Research / Thesis | Soft Teal  | #EAFAF8 | capstone, research, and thesis subjects                   |
| Minor             | Soft Indigo | #EDF0FF | 2-unit subjects and general-education/support subjects    |

## Classification Rules

- `2 units` are treated as `Minor` by default.
- `3 units` are treated as `Major` unless the subject is explicitly general education/support or research/thesis.
- `PT` and `ITELEC` subjects stay `Major (with lab)` when they use lab rooms or lab-oriented subject codes.
- `MS102` remains `Major` because it is a 3-unit BSIT major subject, not a minor.
